Restaurants

We have a variety of restaurant, including:

Ghoulish Diner - enjoy our delicious burgers, pizzas and steaks!

Ghostly Ice Creams and Desserts - satisfy your crave for all things sweet!

All our restaurants include vegan, vegeterian and gluten-free options.

Cafes

Our lovely Cabin cafe offers hot chocolate, tea and, of course, various flavours of coffee!

Hotel Rooms

We provide an array of hotel rooms. You are bound to find a room to spend the night!

A single suite for £70. Enjoy our single suite — spacious and comfortably furnished to ensure that you get your well-deserved night's rest!

A double suite for 90 £. After a scary ride, there's nothing better than curling up ina large bed that's as warm as it is comfortable.

A double lux suite for £110. Grab a bottle of champagne (or water, we don't judge!), and indulge yourself in one of our deluxe rooms which come with free treats and drinks.

Safety

Even if our rides provide thrills, be ensured that your safety is our top priority. For this reason, we offer:

  • An onsite medical booth - our top trained staff can help in the case of an emergency
  • 24 hour security - don't worry, our security staff is always on the watch!
