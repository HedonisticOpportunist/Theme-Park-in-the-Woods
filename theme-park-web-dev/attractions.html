Rides

Our rides are a lot of fun - and hellish too. From the very heights of our rollercoasters to the pits of our cave rides, there is definitely nothing we have not covered. And it is fun for the whole family too, as we also have rides that cater to our slightly younger audiences, too. But beware, you will still be in for a scare!

Haunted House

Why not visit one of our many haunted houses? We have not just got a cabin the woods, but a haunted hotel, a castle and even a family residence that is full of ghouls. Come and visit: our ghosts are dying to meet you!

VR

For all you tech enthusiasts: we have got something special in store for you! Explore worlds that no one has dared before, but make sure to take some ammo! Go where no one has gone before. Embrace the unknown. And be prepared to devoured!

Upcoming Events

Do you want a restaurant of horrors? Go to Freddy's Fazzbear's pizza joint - where animatronics come to life. They may be possessed, we can't be sure!

Weekly Parties

Come and play with us – every week, we have a host of fun parties for you to enjoy.Do you want to meet Dracula? Then join one of our vampire-themed festivities. Or would you rather feel like one of the characters in Silent Hill? No problem, we have got you covered as well. We have a different themed party every week – so there’s definitely something out there to terrify and thrill you.
